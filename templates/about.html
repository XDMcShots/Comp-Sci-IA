Who Are We?

The Green Cycle Drive is a student-led sustainability initiative that started in Dubai and has now grown into an international movement. Through awareness campaigns, recycling projects, and community partnerships, we aim to create a greener, cleaner future.

Our Mission

To empower youth and communities to adopt eco-friendly practices, reduce waste, and influence lasting positive change for our planet.

Our Vision

A world where sustainability is a natural part of everyday life, and where each small action contributes to a brighter, greener tomorrow.
